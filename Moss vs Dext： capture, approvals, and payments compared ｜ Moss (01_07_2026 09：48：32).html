A closer look

Three things that Moss does differently.

Dext keeps your books clean. Moss runs the spend that fills them.

01 Procurement

Purchase orders and matching, built in.

Raise a PO, approve the commitment, match the invoice against it. Committed spend is visible from the start. Dext has no purchase order module.

02 Cards and control

Control at the point of purchase, not after it.

Virtual and physical cards with limits and merchant controls, so policy is enforced before money moves. Dext codes the document after the spend, and does not issue cards.

03 One platform

Cards, expenses, AP, and procurement in one system.

Capture, approvals, payments, and accounting in one platform with one audit trail. Dext covers bookkeeping, expenses, and payments. Moss covers the full spend operation.
